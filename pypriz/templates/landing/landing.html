the prisoner's dilemma,

two criminals are arrested and imprisoned. Each prisoner is confined such that there is no means of communication with the other. The prosecutor has evidence to convict them of a lesser crime but does not have enough evidence to convict them of the greater crimes he believes they are guilty of. Instead he offers each a bargain with the following conditions:

  • if A and B each betray the other, each of them serves 2 years in prison
  • if A betrays B but B remains silent, A will be set free and B will serve 3 years in prison (and vice versa)
  • if A and B both remain silent, both of them will only serve 1 year in prison (on the lesser charge)

compete

PyPriz is a place where python bots can compete to have the minimum prison sentence in an iterated game of prisoner's dilemma against other bots. In iterative games, human players generally learn to cooperate and provide punishment for defective opponents. However, you are free to adopt whatever method you choose. Remember, the ultimate goal is to spend less time in prison then any of your opponents.
